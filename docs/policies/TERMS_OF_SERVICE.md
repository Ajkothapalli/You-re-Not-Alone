## 1. What this service is — and what it is not

You Are Not Alone ("App", "Service") is an anonymous writing and matching
application. You write a private confession. Our system checks it for safety,
then stores it anonymously and attempts to find a semantically similar
confession in the pool. If a match exists, you are shown the matched
confession alongside yours with a count of how many people have felt
something similar.

**This is the complete feature set by design.** There is no direct messaging,
no replies, no profiles, no following, no contact channel between users.
The absence of a reply channel is a deliberate safety feature, not a missing
feature. We will not add one.

**The App is not a crisis service, a mental health service, a counselling
service, or an emergency service.** It does not provide real-time human
monitoring. If you are in immediate danger, contact emergency services.

## 5. Prohibited content

The following content is strictly prohibited. Submissions containing
prohibited content are blocked by our automated pipeline before storage. Your
account may be suspended or permanently terminated for repeated violations.

**Absolute prohibitions (zero tolerance):**

- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any sexual content involving minors.
  Any such submission will result in immediate permanent account termination
  and mandatory reporting to the National Center for Missing and Exploited
  Children (NCMEC) CyberTipline and applicable authorities.

**Prohibited content (moderation enforcement):**

- Hate speech, or content that promotes discrimination based on race,
  ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or national
  origin.
- Threats of violence or incitement to violence against any person or group.
- Harassment, stalking, or content targeting an identifiable real person.
- Sexualised content involving real, identifiable individuals without
  demonstrated consent.
- Graphic violence or gore.
- Content that facilitates or promotes illegal activity.
- Spam, commercial solicitation, or promotional content.
- Content designed to probe or circumvent our safety pipeline, including
  attempts to inject text that manipulates automated classifiers.

Confessions may be written about difficult personal experiences, including
trauma, grief, anxiety, and abuse. Such content is not prohibited. Our
moderation is designed to distinguish personal expression from targeted harm.

## 6. Crisis content

If a submission is identified as indicating a crisis (suicidal ideation,
self-harm, abuse, or severe distress), it will not be published or matched.
You will be shown information about crisis support services.

We take this routing seriously. We do not consider it a substitute for
professional help. **If you are in crisis, please contact emergency services
or a crisis helpline.** The App cannot call for help on your behalf.
